~ a dream in art, design, and living well ~

The House of Things Gallery is both a journey and a destination — an immersive 25,000-square-foot space dedicated to thoughtfully curated art, furniture, homeware, and rare collectibles from across the world.

Each piece is purposefully designed and rich with heritage. By exploring the stories behind these objects, we uncover fragments of a home — woven together through craft, history, and timeless design.
